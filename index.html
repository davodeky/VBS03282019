Sunday's Sermon

Disciplines For The Disciple

5. The Discipline of Fellowship

Introduction
We have thus far looked at the disciplines of:

  • prayer- secret, simple, and steadfast.
  • Meditation- contemplating God, His works, His words, and things worthy of virtue.
  • fasting- humbling oneself before God when joined with sincere prayer.
  • singing- which edifies the singer as well as praises God.
  • Fellowship- communing with other Christians as we engage in spiritual activities.
  • Being afraid is not the lack of faith.
  • We all have to face fear at some point.
  • It is courage that makes us reach out for faith when faced with fear.

This week we are looking at the discipline of Giving:
  1. Not just in the sense of giving money to someone in need.
  2. But in giving of one's time, energy, or abilities, especially where it involves sacrifice.
Such giving is certainly demonstrated in the New Testament:
  1. By the example of the churches of Macedonia - 2 Corinthians 8:1-5
  2. By the example of Christ Himself - 2 Corinthians 8:9

Questions
  1. How should we give?
  2. What are the benefits of giving?
